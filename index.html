Questions

Everything owners
usually ask us.

How fast will my website be live?

Seven days from our first conversation, for most businesses. You see a working demo by day 3, and nothing goes live until you approve it.

Do I own my website and domain?

Yes. The domain is registered in your name, always. If you ever leave, you keep the domain and we hand over your content — no hostage situations.

What if I already have a website?

We'll rebuild it properly, migrate your content and domain, and usually improve your Google ranking in the process. Redesigns are included in the monthly price.

Why monthly instead of one big payment?

Because a website isn't a one-time thing — it needs hosting, updates, security and fresh content. One monthly price covers all of it, and keeps us motivated to keep your site working hard.

Do you work with businesses outside your city?

Yes — we work with clients across India, the United States and Canada. Everything can be done over video calls and chat; you'll never feel the distance.

How do payments work?

UPI autopay, cards or bank transfer — whatever suits you. Annual plans skip the setup fee.
